DOCapp

I was originally completing a course on UX designing.

So as apart of a assignment i have done this project.

Objective

SO this was my brain child which i shaped into reality converting it into UI.

The aim was to build a more intuitive and smarter system to book appointment from Doctors, book slots for pathology tests, to check availabity of medicines in medical stores to facilitate a better experience for customers and users.

My Role

I have shaped the whole thing from scratch(from brainstorming to UI) as an Interaction/UI designers.

Challenges

  • It is was fun to create this.
  • As i was working alone, it was difficult to complete it in time. But i learnt time management.
  • I missed team-work.

Design Decisions

Linear journeys

To create linear customer journeys from a seemingly non-existent flow - presenting efficient service to customer who are busy in their life.

Contextual information

To reduce the waste of time that people usually spent on getting an appointment from doctors by going to the doctors place.

Feedback

Postive feedback received from diffent peoples, still needs improvement and i am working on it.

Automation

The existing system requires most tasks to be performed manually, and sometimes multiple times.

Results

People can use this app whenever it comes into reality.

Phase 1 designs have been done and is currently in discussion with my developers. Release is not confirmed.
